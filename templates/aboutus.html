About MedTrack

MedTrack is a cloud-based application developed to help patients manage their medications and appointments efficiently. Designed with user-friendliness in mind, it offers seamless interaction between patients and doctors.

Our key features include:

  • Secure login and profile management for both doctors and patients
  • Appointment booking with real-time doctor dashboard
  • Doctor-to-patient prescription and diagnosis communication
  • Smart scheduling with prevention of past-date selections

Whether you're a patient managing chronic treatments or a doctor coordinating with multiple patients, MedTrack is here to support better health outcomes through smart digital tools.
